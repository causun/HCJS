Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed bt an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, interveing film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the the main action or plot os a film; contrast to epilogue
